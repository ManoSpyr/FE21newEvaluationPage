Stanley Kubrick
July 26, 1928 – March 7, 1999
A filmaking mastermind

An American film director, producer, screenwriter, and photographer.
He is frequently cited as one of the greatest filmmakers in cinematic history.
His films, which are mostly adaptations of novels or short stories,
cover a wide range of genres and are noted for their realism, dark humor,
unique cinematography,extensive set designs, and evocative use of music.
